Deployed Site: Erudition Social Learning App

Erudition is a social networking platform that focuses on a following system to build its internal content of information. It was built using React Flux for state-management, Google authentication through Firebase, Firestore database, Next.js for routing/server side rendering/code-splitting, and Material-UI for CSS/design.

Erudition also has a lot of scraping behind the scenes to pull data from articles, blogs, and other media websites to display on our app.
