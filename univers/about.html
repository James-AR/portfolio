03 : About me

Full Stack Web Developer

I will always remember the exact day in which I began my journey as a full stack web developer. That is because it happened to coincide with my 25th birthday in 2018, the day I was accepted full-time into Lambda School. During this journey I evolved from having zero previous experience, to becoming code savvy enough to the extent of being able to create fully fledged applications utilizing a wide variety tools, libraries, and frameworks.

Curious Mind

I am fascinated of the world; particularly cultures, cuisines, health, destinations, and of course, people. There is a dichotomy within myself: one that seeks adventure to continuously make new discoveries of the world, and one that embraces sitting comfortably at a computer into the world of code. The key to my happiness is the balance between the two.
